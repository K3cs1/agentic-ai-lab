Who can apply?
Permanent Accenture employees based in Romania, are expected to have an American Express (Amex) Corporate Card and use the card
for all reimbursable expenses, wherever the card is accepted in line with Policy 1396.an American Express (Amex) Corporate Card.
Your Amex card should be used for business related expenses only – personal expenditure is not allowed at any time.

How to apply?
Please ensure you obtain pre-approval from your project lead or line manager (minimum management level 6 or above) before applying
for a new Amex corporate card. Managers or above must secure approval from someone at a higher management level.
This is a mandatory requirement, and the approval will be verified by your local Amex Program Administrator.

SEPA Direct Debit Mandate is strongly recommended to be completed, as direct debit is the most efficient method of payment, to ensure payments on date and avoid late fees or unpaid balances. If not completed, please ensure to make manual payments at the end of each billing cycle.

You will receive your card 3-4 weeks after you've applied, subject to postal delays.

If you are visually impaired, your local Amex Program Administrator can help you complete the application form. Please contact Ask Mobility.
